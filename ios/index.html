Ambulance for iOS

Install or update the iOS app. Current version: 0.3.

Version 0.3 Released May 03, 2026
Download iOS App Installation Guide

Setup steps

  1. Open this page on your iPhone, preferably in Safari.
  2. Tap Download iOS App.
  3. When iOS asks to download a configuration profile, tap Allow, then tap Close after Profile Downloaded appears.
  4. Open Settings, go to General, then VPN & Device Management.
  5. Under Downloaded Profile, tap Ambulance App, then tap Install.
  6. Enter your iPhone passcode if prompted, confirm Install, then tap Done. The app appears in your App Library or Home Screen.
