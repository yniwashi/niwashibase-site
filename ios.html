Install guide

The iOS app installs directly to your iPhone Home Screen in four steps. Must be done in Safari.

  1. Open the link below in Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow) at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add — the app appears on your Home Screen immediately.
Get iOS App Watch install video

Other browsers do not support Add to Home Screen — Safari only.
