What the app helps with

Ambulance brings app tools, searchable references, timers, calculators, and operational shortcuts into one place.

Search and references

Find app tools, guidelines, flowcharts, formulary content, and document sections from one place.

Timers and clinical tools

Use high-level app tools such as CPR, RSI Checklist, WAAFELSS, scores, pediatric tools, and ventilator settings.

Operations

Access Shift Schedule, Overtime, Websites, Report Issue, and support links.
