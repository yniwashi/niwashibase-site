v2.1 Latest Android Released May 27, 2026
  • New access flow for Ambulance staff and other users.
  • Redesigned home screen with grouped sections, notices, and clearer dashboard layout.
  • Major CPR Timer rebuild with background support, audio cues, history, export, and backup/restore.
  • CPG v2.5, rebuilt search, Guidelines PDF text search, and qSOFA.
  • New Shift Schedule, Visual Guides, Burn Surface Area, and Adult Ventilator Settings.
  • Updated pediatric tools, Overtime, AS-Call, HOS, Websites, and Report Issue.
Read more

Access and home

  • New Ambulance Staff access flow.
  • New Other User access flow with limited supported features.
  • Personalized home screen greeting.
  • New dashboard design with clearer sections, updated colors, spacing, and layout.
  • Greeting banner can be changed and has a subtle tap animation.
  • Notice bell now shows unread Notices.

CPR Timer

  • CPR timer can continue while the app is in the background or the phone is locked.
  • CPR notification added with quick actions: Start/Pause, Shock, and ROSC.
  • CPR introduction screens added for first-time users and can be replayed from Settings.
  • New CPR settings and fresh layout.
  • Improved adrenaline prompt workflow.
  • New CPR audio with Female and Male voices.
  • Audio reminders added for adrenaline and amiodarone, with amiodarone reminders after the 3rd and 5th shocks.
  • Clearer action feedback, CPR history, event timeline, PDF/CSV/JSON export, backup/restore, and improved ROSC restart handling.

Clinical tools

  • WAAFELSS display, concentration wording, CPR layout, pediatric tube-size display, rounding, and validation improved.
  • qSOFA added and Scores now use a cleaner dropdown selector.
  • Score order updated: GCS, APGAR, SAT, qSOFA, Westley, PGCS.
  • Shift Schedule added with Team Calendar, Date Lookup, and Coverage tools.
  • Pediatric medication screens improved with normal vitals, better layout, concentration, and draw-up volume display.
  • Adult Ventilator Settings added with Normal Lungs, Asthma/COPD, and APO/ARDS.

Operations and calculators

  • Visual Guides added for Defibrillation Pad Placement, ECG Lead Placement, and Burn Surface Area.
  • Burn Surface Area Calculator added for adult, child, and infant body diagrams.
  • Search and Guidelines improved with better result cards, faster navigation, and PDF search.
  • Overtime rebuilt with salary saving, history, filters, exports, backup/restore, and normal/Ramadan calculations.
  • AS-Call, HOS, and Websites improved with call confirmation, HOS behavior updates, website icons, search, and filters.
  • Report Issue redesigned with problem type, app area, optional description, support report sharing, and optional support contact number.
  • In-app Notices, App Intro, smoother startup, older-device support, improved layouts, better button feedback, bug fixes, and reliability improvements.
